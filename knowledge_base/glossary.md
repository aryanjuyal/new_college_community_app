**Lost & Found Post:**  
A report of a lost or found item visible to all users.

**Status (Lost / Found):**  
Indicates if the item is still missing or already found.

**Community Posts:**  
General discussion or update posts shared by students or societies.

**Event Registration:**  
Joining an event within the app to attend or participate.

**Society Page:**  
Official profile of a college club or society, showing their activities.

**AI Moderation:**  
An automated system that flags or hides inappropriate content.

**Post Recommendation:**  
Feature suggesting posts relevant to your interests or previous activity.
