# Events & Societies in Community App

The app keeps students informed about all ongoing and upcoming events.

## Viewing Events
- Tap the **Events** section to see upcoming college events.
- You can filter by club, date, or category.

## Joining / Registering
- Tap on any event → Click **Register / Join Event**.
- You’ll receive confirmation inside the app.

## Creating Events
- **Societies and clubs** or **authorized users** can post events.
- The event must be **valid** (verified by admins).

## Event Details
Each event shows:
- Event Name  
- Organized by (Club/Society/Dept)
- Date and Time  
- Venue  
- Description

## Club & Society Pages
- Clubs and societies have their own public pages.
- All users can **view** these pages and see their posts.
- Only **members** can **post** or manage content on those pages.
