# App Navigation – Community App

The **Community App** is your college’s all-in-one platform for connecting with clubs, societies, and peers.  
It helps students stay updated with events, report lost items, and share posts.

## Main Sections
- **Lost & Found** → Report or search for lost and found items.
- **Posts / Discussions** → View and share college updates, discussions, and announcements.
- **Events** → Discover upcoming college events and register directly.
- **Future Score Prediction** → View your academic insights and growth metrics.

## How to Access
- Open the app and sign in with your **College ID**.
- Use the **bottom navigation bar** to switch between sections.

## Quick Tips
- Use the **+** button in Community to create new posts.
- Tap your **profile icon** to access settings and feedback forms.
