# Community Posts and Discussions

The **Posts** section allows students and societies to share updates, discussions, and announcements.

## Types of Posts
- **General Discussions**: Ask questions, share thoughts, or discuss campus news.
- **Society / Club Posts**: Official updates or announcements from societies.
- **Event Promotions**: Upcoming events or activities.

## Interactions
- Like and comment on posts.
- Report posts that break community rules.

## Moderation Rules
To maintain a positive environment:
- ❌ No vulgar, offensive, or disrespectful language.
- ❌ No spam or irrelevant advertisements.
- ✅ Keep posts college-relevant and respectful.

Posts flagged by users are **reviewed by admins**.  
AI moderation also helps detect offensive or inappropriate content automatically.

## Post Recommendation Feature
The app recommends posts to users based on:
- Their **interests** listed in their profile.
- Their **past interactions** with posts.
